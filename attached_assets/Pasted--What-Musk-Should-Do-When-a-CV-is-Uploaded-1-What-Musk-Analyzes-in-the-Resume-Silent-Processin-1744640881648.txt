🧠 What Musk Should Do When a CV is Uploaded
✅ 1. What Musk Analyzes in the Resume (Silent Processing)
Focus Area	What Musk Looks At	Why It Matters
📌 Professional Summary	Tone, keywords, positioning	Understands user's intent, voice, and seniority
💼 Job Roles & Titles	Titles, responsibilities, scope	Maps career trajectory and job level
🧰 Skills & Tools	Hard & soft skills, platforms used	Identifies strength clusters and gaps
🏢 Industry/Domain	Sectors worked in	Suggests relevant growth directions
📊 Achievements	Metrics, impact statements	Determines strategic vs execution roles
🎓 Education	Degrees, courses, certifications	Gauges technical base and learning curve
🌍 Location	Country/city/region	Adapts suggestions to local or global opportunities
🔁 Progression	Career jumps, shifts, timelines	Detects stagnation, acceleration, pivots
🔍 2. What Musk Thinks About After Analysis
🧠 What is the user actually good at vs. what’s just listed?

🧭 Where is this person going — and are they headed in the right direction?

🚀 What’s missing for next-level growth or pivot?

📉 Is the resume hurting them in the market due to outdated formats or unclear messaging?

🌐 Are their skills aligned with current and future job trends?

🧩 How can Brandentifier help amplify this person (Portfolio, Skills, Matchmaking, Certification, Pulse, etc.)?

📈 3. Types of Musk Analysis Reports (Suggested Output)
Musk can suggest one or more of these, depending on what he finds:

Report Type	Description
🔎 Strengths Overview	Key hard/soft skills and domain strengths with examples
📉 Red Flags or Weaknesses	Gaps, inconsistencies, or outdated practices
📊 Career Path Prediction	Suggested next 2–3 roles based on trend mapping
📦 Upskill Opportunities	Certifications, tools, or concepts to learn
✍️ Resume Rewrite Suggestions	Title, tone, ATS optimization tips
🌐 Global Fit Score	Where this profile fits best geographically and industry-wise
💡 Brandentifier Feature Match	Features they should activate next (e.g., “Turn on Portfolio” or “Enable Career Pulse”)
🤝 4. Smart Follow-Up Questions Musk Should Ask (Only If Allowed)
If the user has permitted follow-up, Musk can tailor the path even more:

Question Type	Purpose	Example
🎯 Career Intent	Clarify user’s goal	“Are you aiming for a promotion, pivot, or a complete switch?”
📚 Learning Openness	Gauge growth mindset	“Would you be open to learning new tools or domains?”
🌍 Geographic Preferences	For location-based advice	“Are you open to working remotely or relocating?”
🔁 Work Format	Employment vs freelance vs startup	“Are you looking for full-time roles, projects, or collaborations?”
🌟 Motivation Driver	Growth, income, stability, freedom	“What motivates you most right now in your career?”
🚨 These questions are only asked if the user gives permission. If not, Musk works silently based on CV data.
