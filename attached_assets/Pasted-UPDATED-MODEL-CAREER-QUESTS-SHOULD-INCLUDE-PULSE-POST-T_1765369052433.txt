UPDATED MODEL: CAREER QUESTS SHOULD INCLUDE PULSE POST TYPES

Career Quests will now include 3 types of Pulse post suggestions:

🔵 1. Profile-Based Pulse Posts

These are posts generated based on missing or weak profile sections.

Examples:

If UVP is generic → Pulse: “This is what I uniquely do in my industry…”

If industry/domain not clear → Pulse: “Here’s what I actually specialize in…”

If About Me too short → Pulse: “My journey so far…”

Sample Quest:
Title: Clarify Your Expertise with a Pulse Post
Description: Write a Pulse post that explains what you do, why you do it, and the value you bring.
PulseType: Identity Post
EstimatedTime: 15

🟣 2. Authority Pulse Posts

These help users prove their expertise inside the Brandentifier ecosystem.

Types:

Insight Post (share a lesson learned)

Case Study Post (mini-project outcome)

Tips Post (3 tips from your field)

Opinion Post (your perspective on industry trend)

Mistakes Post (things beginners get wrong)

Behind-the-Scenes Post (your workflow)

Sample Quest:
Title: Publish an Authority Pulse Post
Description: Share one insight or lesson from your industry. Keep it punchy and valuable.
PulseType: Authority Post
EstimatedTime: 20

🔥 3. Opportunity Pulse Posts

These posts help the user express what they are looking for.

Examples:

“I’m open for freelance projects”

“I’m looking for collaboration partners”

“I’m currently seeking roles in XYZ domain”

“Here’s what I’m working on this week — open for networking”

Sample Quest:
Title: Share a Pulse About What You're Looking For
Description: Tell your audience what kind of opportunities or collaborations you're open to.
PulseType: Opportunity Post
EstimatedTime: 10

🎯 STRUCTURE: Every Career Quest That Mentions "Pulse" Should Include:

PulseType (Identity / Authority / Opportunity)

Template (a pre-written fill-in-the-blank)

Tone (professional / friendly / bold / minimalist)

Optional hashtags (only internal, not for external)

MuskTip (motivational insight)

🧠 HOW THE SYSTEM DECIDES WHICH PULSE TYPE TO SUGGEST

Based on:

1️⃣ Missing field → Identity Pulse
2️⃣ Weak profile → Authority Pulse
3️⃣ LookingFor is filled → Opportunity Pulse
4️⃣ User is job-seeking → Authority + Opportunity
5️⃣ User is freelancer → Authority + Case Study
6️⃣ User is creator → Behind-the-scenes + Tips

No UI changes needed — this is purely backend logic.
