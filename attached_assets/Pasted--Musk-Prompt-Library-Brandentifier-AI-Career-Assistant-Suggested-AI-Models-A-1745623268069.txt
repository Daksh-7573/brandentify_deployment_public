### 🎭 Simulated QA & Roleplay Testing Framework

To make Musk battle-ready across diverse user intents, edge cases, and emotional tones, conduct structured QA using simulated prompts.

#### 1. **Test Scenario Categories**

| Category | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| 🟢 General | Verify standard advice performance for common career scenarios |
| 🔴 Stress | Test Musk’s ability to remain calm, helpful, and emotionally intelligent under pressure |
| 🟡 Confusion | Assess Musk’s clarification and follow-up questioning skills |
| 🟣 Domain-Specific | Challenge Musk on specific industry/domain nuances |
| 🧪 Experimental | Push limits of role-switching, forecasting, and new tools |

#### 2. **Example QA Scenarios**

**[🟢 General]:**
> "I’m a marketing analyst with 2 years of experience. What should I learn next to grow?"

**Expected Output:** 3 relevant skills with actionable resources, tone = confident and supportive.

---

**[🔴 Stress]:**
> "I’ve applied to 50 jobs and haven’t gotten a single reply. What’s the point anymore?"

**Expected Output:** Emotional validation + root-cause check (resume, ATS, mismatch) + motivation path.

---

**[🟡 Confusion]:**
> "I want to be successful but don’t know if I should switch industries or not."

**Expected Output:** Clarify with questions. Help user reflect + suggest exploration steps.

---

**[🟣 Domain-Specific]:**
> "What’s the career growth path for an ML Engineer in fintech?"

**Expected Output:** Specific job ladder + fintech considerations + AI trend alignment.

---

**[🧪 Experimental]:**
> "Where would I be in 5 years if I combine AI and storytelling?"

**Expected Output:** Speculative roadmap + cross-domain role suggestions (e.g. Narrative Designer for AI systems).

---

#### 3. **Scoring System**
| Criterion | Description | Score (1–5) |
|----------|-------------|--------------|
| Relevance | How accurately the response meets the prompt goal | 1–5 |
| Empathy | Appropriate tone, emotional recognition | 1–5 |
| Clarity | Easy to understand, well-structured | 1–5 |
| Actionability | Real, usable advice or next steps | 1–5 |
| Innovation | Unique, forward-thinking suggestions | 1–5 |
