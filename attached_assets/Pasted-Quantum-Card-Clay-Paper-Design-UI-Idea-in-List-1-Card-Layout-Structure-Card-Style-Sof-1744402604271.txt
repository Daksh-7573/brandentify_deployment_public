Quantum Card – Clay & Paper Design (UI Idea in List)
🧱 1. Card Layout & Structure
 Card Style:

Soft shadowed card (paper feel) layered on a clay-style surface

Elevation via multiple card layers (Z-depth)

Rounded corners (16–20px), paper-like edges

 Card Dimensions:

~360x500 px, vertical layout

Responsive for web & mobile (scales down to 100%)

👤 2. Profile Identity Section
 Profile Picture

Slightly elevated circle avatar

Paper-cut-style shadow

Subtle inner border or embossed effect

 Name

Large serif or rounded sans-serif font (e.g., Satoshi, Poppins)

Text style: paper emboss or “cutout look”

 Job Title

Under name, in lighter font weight

Soft clay-toned text on white background

🧩 3. Professional Details Section
 Industry / Domain

Shown as flat tags or paper-label-style chips

Example: #Product, #Marketing, #Design

 Company / Organization

🏢 Icon + name in a light shaded box (paper block style)

Optional: company logo inside embossed mini circle

 Location

📍 Icon with city/state

Horizontal separator line (faint dashed line like paper fold)

📬 4. Contact Information
 Email Address

📧 Left-aligned with soft icon

Background: pale paper tile or clay chip

 Phone Number

📞 Similar style, click-to-call (if on mobile)

Optional copy icon next to contact

🔐 Visibility of this section controlled via user’s privacy settings.

🎨 5. Visual Style & Aesthetics
 Material Style:

Combines soft clay colors (pastel blue, blush, taupe) with clean white or off-white paper cards

Background blur or soft texture (like Japanese Washi paper)

 Shadow Effects:

Inner shadows for card layers (like stacked paper)

Outer soft clay drop shadows

 Font Suggestion:

Poppins, Nunito, or Sora for modern clay/paper vibe

Slight letter-spacing for clean readability

🧠 6. Interactivity & Animation
 Subtle float on hover

 Button ripple or ink-style click feedback (like Google Material Design)

 Tap/click reveals full profile or connects to chat

🔧 7. Optional Features
 Download or Print button (for printable version of card)

 “Send My Card” feature with a soft fly-out animation

 Minimal icons to share or bookmark profile
