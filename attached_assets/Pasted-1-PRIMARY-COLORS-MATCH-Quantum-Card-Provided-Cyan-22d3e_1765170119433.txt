🧠 Why It Looks Different

In your original spec, colors were spread across the document in context:

Glows

Shadows

Backgrounds

Borders

I reorganized them into:

Primary palette

Background palette

Text palette

Border/glow palette

This is called a design token system, and it helps:

Designers apply consistent styling

Devs convert to Tailwind / CSS vars

Theming / dark mode scalability

But the raw color values are identical.
