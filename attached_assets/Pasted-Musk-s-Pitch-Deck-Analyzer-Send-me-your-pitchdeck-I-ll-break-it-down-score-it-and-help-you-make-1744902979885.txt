Musk’s Pitch Deck Analyzer
“Send me your pitchdeck — I’ll break it down, score it, and help you make it world-class.”

🧠 What Musk Does:
When a user uploads their Pitch Deck (PDF), Musk:

Reads slide-by-slide using OCR + GPT

Understands structure, storytelling, flow

Assesses clarity, persuasiveness, and investor readiness

Scores and identifies gaps

Suggests direct, detailed improvements per slide

Optionally offers rewrite or reframe suggestions

Recommends layout or visual fixes if weak

🔍 What Musk Looks For (Analysis Matrix)

Slide Type	What Musk Evaluates
🧑‍💼 Problem Slide	Is the pain point clear, emotional, and quantified?
💡 Solution	Is the solution differentiated, simple, and compelling?
🏗️ Product	Is it demoed clearly? Is the core feature shown?
📈 Market Size	Is TAM/SAM/SOM included? Is it realistic and data-backed?
⚔️ Competition	Is there a landscape matrix? Is your edge clear?
💰 Business Model	Can we see how you make money, clearly?
👥 Team	Are roles, background, and credibility covered?
📊 Traction	Real numbers? Growth? Milestones? Roadmap?
💸 Ask Slide	Are the funding goals, use of funds, and timeline defined?
🎯 Vision	Does it end with ambition, purpose, and call to action?
