Quantum Card – Artistic Design (UI Idea in List Format)
🧱 1. Layout & Style Concept
 Canvas-Inspired Layout:

Abstract frame layout — like a digital scrapbook or artist’s board

Layered, hand-drawn elements blended with subtle animations

Irregular edge shapes, soft gradients, and paper-textured backgrounds

 Card Shape:

Organic silhouette (not rectangular), feels handmade

Slight color splashes or brush strokes as part of the border design

👤 2. Profile Picture
 Displayed as a circular or artistic cut-out shape (e.g., blob, brush-circle)

 Frame with pencil sketch or paint stroke border

 Optional animated glow or tilt when hovered

✍️ 3. Name & Job Title
 Name

Stylized with a script or serif font (e.g., DM Serif, Playfair)

May sit on a paint label or abstract sticker background

 Job Title

Offset placement (diagonally across or under name)

Slight animation or fade-in from behind brush stroke on hover

🧩 4. Professional Details
 Industry / Domain

Displayed as handwritten-style tags or paint chip labels

Example: #Photography, #Illustration, #Storytelling

 Company / Organization

Placed inside a badge-style “exhibition label”

Optional paper-textured container with company logo or initials

 Location

Marker icon + handwritten city/state

Placed on a mini location pin graphic or subtle map sketch background

✉️ 5. Contact Information
 Email Address

📧 Icon on a floating “post-it note” or folded paper tab

Hover → note flips to reveal full email + copy option

 Phone Number

📞 Appears as a stamped label or clay-style button

Vibrates or glows lightly when hovered

Contact info is only shown if visibility is enabled by the user

🌈 6. Artistic Theme & Visuals
 Textures: Watercolor stains, light canvas fabric, handmade paper

 Colors: Earth tones, rich pastels, creative gradients (mint, rust, lilac, ink blue)

 Typography: Pairing script or serif fonts for names with clean sans-serif body

🧠 7. Motion & Interactions
 Subtle parallax when moving the card

 Hover glow on image and name

 Ink ripple animation on action buttons (like “Connect” or “View Full Profile”)

 Elements fade in like layers of an art piece (top-down)

🎯 Bonus Creative Touches
 Brush stroke line separators between sections

 Floating color palette dots behind the card

 Hand-drawn signature-style name option

 “Portfolio” or “Gallery” button shown as paintbrush icon or folded canvas
