🧠 Musk’s Multi-Layered Resume Improvement Engine (Advanced Training Plan)
✅ 1. Train Musk on Bullet-Level Intelligence
Goal	Musk Should Learn to Detect	Example Improvement
Weak phrasing	"Responsible for managing..."	→ “Led a team of 5 to execute 3+ marketing campaigns with 24% average ROI”
Missing metrics	Vague verbs like “worked on”, “helped”	→ Suggest numbers: “Optimized campaign → drove 15% engagement”
Fluff or filler	“Various tasks including…”	→ Rewrite into clear impact-based statements
Passive language	“Tasked with...” or “involved in…”	→ Use active: “Initiated”, “Led”, “Achieved”
🎯 Train on 1000+ real bullet point rewrites for high-precision recommendations.

🧠 2. Advanced Section-Level Suggestions
Section	Musk Should Check	Suggestions
Summary	Does it capture expertise, value, future intent?	Rewrite for clarity, confidence & industry focus
Experience	Is it reverse chronological, high impact, easy to skim?	Suggest trimming old roles, bullet formatting, adding projects
Skills	Are keywords missing or unordered?	Reorder by relevance, add in-demand tools
Education	Are key achievements listed?	Suggest “Dean’s list”, %/GPA, or key projects
Certifications	Are they relevant or outdated?	Highlight most valuable, hide expired or redundant ones
