🧠 New Concept Name:
🎨 “Layered Identity Canvas”
A tactile, dimensional, and scrollable mini-profile board — like a digital collage of the person, made from soft paper, waxed clay, and floating elements.

🔷 Core Concept Highlights:
✂️ Inspired by artisanal paper-cut styles and tactile clay models

🧩 Feels like a physical board you can touch, peel, or explore

📐 Visually divided into folded sections and interactive pieces

💫 Modern minimalist meets handmade aesthetics

📄 Canvas Layout (Section by Section)
🧍‍♂️ 1. Profile Block (Top Left, Pinned Like a Polaroid)
 Profile Picture

Looks like a physical photo taped/pinned to the canvas

Tilted slightly (like it's pasted manually)

Subtle shadow & elevation

 Full Name + Job Title

Rendered as hand-stamped text or printed label

Slight offset shadow like it's stuck on separately

🏷️ 2. Layered Tag Swatches (Bottom of Name Area)
 Industry / Domain Tags

Clay-colored fabric-like ribbons

Floating above or slightly hanging from the main name layer

Glows or stretches slightly on hover

🏢 3. Current Role Block (Folded Card Style)
 Company Name

Appears like a folded sticky note or open letter

Clicking expands more job details (timeline, projects)

Clay button: “See Role History” (launches vertical scroll modal)

 Location

Small paper plane or map marker in clay pressed style

✉️ 4. Contact Strip (Side-Stitched or Tear-Off Tab)
 Email + Phone

On a perforated card strip on right side

Has “tear-off” look (like coupons or tickets)

Hover = peel animation

Click = copy or action

🌫️ 5. Background & Texture Ideas
 Light canvas background with subtle grain

 Visual layers of clay & paper sheets stacked

 Floating dust particles for subtle depth

🎨 Color Scheme & Typography
Element	Style Idea
Background	Soft white + pastel clay (taupe, nude, sage, mist blue)
Font Style	Retro-modern: Sora, Space Grotesk, or Poppins
Icon Style	Custom outline set with hand-sketched feel
Interaction Color	Paper yellow, blush coral, graphite black, muted lilac
🪄 Interactive Touches
 Tilt/Shadow based on cursor

 Click-to-flip for backside info (QR code, services, social)

 Wax seal animation when connecting or sending a message

 Ripped-paper reveal transition when switching views

🔧 Optional Add-Ons
 Mini paper clip near picture = quick connect

 Floating Post-it notes = endorsements or testimonials

 Stamp icon if profile is verified or recommended
