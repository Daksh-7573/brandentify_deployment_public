### 📈 Trend Graph + Job Graph Training

Train Musk to understand **macro and micro career data** patterns by creating dynamic graphs for decision-making.

#### 1. **Trend Graph: Skills & Industry Forecasting**
| Feature | Use |
|---------|-----|
| Skill Velocity Tracker | Track which skills are growing fastest in a domain (e.g. "Prompt Engineering" in AI) |
| Industry Direction Chart | Detect shifts (e.g. Fintech → GreenTech jobs) and suggest pivot paths |
| Micro-trend Surfacing | Show short-term spikes (e.g. “Remote Brand Strategist” roles) |

> **Data Sources:** LinkedIn trends, Coursera enrollment spikes, job APIs, Twitter/X trend analysis, Stack Overflow tags.

#### 2. **Job Graph: Role Transition Mapping**
| Feature | Use |
|---------|-----|
| Skill-Connected Role Graph | Connect user’s current role to future roles via overlapping skills |
| Title Pathway Predictor | Predict job titles the user can realistically aim for in 1–3–5 year intervals |
| Salary Trajectory Guide | Overlay compensation ranges on each possible job node |

> **Data Sources:** O*NET, Glassdoor, Levels.fyi, GitHub, Indeed, user journeys on Brandentifier

#### 3. **Musk Capabilities Using These Graphs**
- Suggest “Roles You May Not Have Considered”
- Highlight when a skill becomes obsolete or hot
- Recommend micro-pivots vs full career jumps
- Auto-recommend which graph to view based on user goal

#### 4. **UI/UX Suggestions for Graph Display**
- Interactive force-directed graph: Node = role, Link = skill
- Timeline heatmap for skill trends over 6–12–24 months
- “Compare Pathways” side-by-side transition cards
