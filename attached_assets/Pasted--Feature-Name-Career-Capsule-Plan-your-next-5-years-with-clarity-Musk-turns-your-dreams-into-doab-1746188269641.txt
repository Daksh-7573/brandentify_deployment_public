 Feature Name: Career Capsule
“Plan your next 5 years with clarity. Musk turns your dreams into doable timelines.”

🎯 Core Objective
Help users break down long-term career goals into actionable yearly capsules, guided by Musk.

🧠 Feature Concept Breakdown
🪐 Step 1: Create Your Capsule
User chooses a timeframe: 1–5 years

Gives their career vision (text or choose from dropdown goals)

Examples:

“Become a Product Manager in a top company”

“Launch my own marketing agency”

“Work remotely in Canada”

“Get promoted to VP-level”

“Transition to AI/ML domain”

🧩 Step 2: Musk Timeline Builder
Musk analyzes:

User’s current profile, skills, gaps

Industry & domain shifts

Their selected goal

Then auto-generates a Career Timeline broken into yearly Capsules:


Year	Title	Steps
Year 1	Skill Foundation	Learn X, take Y course, update resume
Year 2	Domain Positioning	Publish content, network with Z, join forums
Year 3	Apply Strategically	Build case studies, pitch to companies
Year 4	Achieve Milestone	Apply to dream job/start venture
Year 5	Elevate Brand	Mentor others, grow team, public speaking
Each step has a ✅ button — users mark completed as they go.

🧭 Step 3: Interactive Tracker
A vertical or horizontal visual timeline (with animations)

Musk provides nudges:

“You're behind on Year 2. Want a catch-up plan?”

“You completed Year 1 early! Let’s accelerate Year 2?”

Tasks can be edited/added manually too.

🌟 Step 4: Gamification & Rewards

Action	Reward
Completing a year	Badge + +10 Brand Score
Completing all goals	Career Capsule Completion Trophy (digital)
Sharing success	Optional public post/Pulse with story
📱 UI Ideas

Section	Visual
Goal Setup	Type + select multiple goals + timeline slider (1–5y)
Timeline View	Scrollable capsule with progress bars and Musk tips
Task Cards	Micro cards per step: “Start AI Foundations Course”, “Publish case study”
Dashboard Widget	“You’re 40% through Year 2 of your Capsule”
Journal Entries (Optional)	Log learnings and progress reflections
🔒 Privacy
By default: private

Option to share Capsule with mentor or network

Option to show Capsule progress badge on public profile

🎯 Smart Suggestions
🎯 “Convert a completed Capsule into a Case Study Pulse”

🧠 “You’re trending behind — want Musk to replan with 1 extra year?”

🔁 Allow users to run multiple Capsules (ex: 1 for career, 1 for business, 1 for study abroad)
