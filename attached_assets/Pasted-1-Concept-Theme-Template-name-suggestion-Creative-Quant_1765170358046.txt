1️⃣ Concept & Theme

Template name (suggestion):

“Creative Quantum Portfolio”
Best for: Designers, developers, marketers, creative professionals.

Visual vibe:

Dark, tech-inspired, high-end

Same as the Creative Quantum Card: grid overlay, circuit SVG, chip SVG, cyan/purple glow

Feels like: a full-page version of the card – not a disconnected layout

Core rule:

The card becomes the base component – the portfolio is like a stack of quantum sections, each built as a wide, stretched version of this card.

2️⃣ Page Layout Overview

Vertical scroll layout with 7 major sections:

Hero Header – “Creative Quantum Card” expanded

About / Snapshot – your professional story

Skills Grid – listed in a creative, glowing layout

Experience Timeline – your work journey

Education Timeline

Projects Gallery – cases with hover overlays

Services (Premium) – pricing / offerings

Each section:

Lives inside a full-width background with:

Dark gradient (0A0F2C → 1F1B44)

Grid overlay

SVG circuit/chip patterns in corners

Has a glassmorphism inner card following your Creative Quantum Card style.
