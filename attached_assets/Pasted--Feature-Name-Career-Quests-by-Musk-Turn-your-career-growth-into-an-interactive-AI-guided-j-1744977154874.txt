 Feature Name: “Career Quests” by Musk
🎯 "Turn your career growth into an interactive, AI-guided journey."

🧠 What is Career Quests?
It’s a gamified, AI-personalized mission system inside Brandentifier that:
Gives each user weekly or monthly quests


Based on their profile, goal, industry, and behavior


Includes micro-challenges like:


“Post your first Insight Pulse this week”


“Comment on 3 trending Pulses in your domain”


“Connect with a mentor”


“Update your portfolio with one new project”


“Rewrite your bio using Musk's suggestion”


“React to a post with 🔥 Insightful”


And in return, they earn:
🚀 XP (Growth Points)


🧠 Skill Badges


🎖️ Portfolio Boost Stickers


🌐 “Discover Me” mode (get featured on Wall)


🔓 Unlock new Musk features or portfolio layouts



🔄 How It Works (Flow)
User logs in → Musk says:


 "Hi Nishant! Based on your goal to 'Get More Freelance Gigs', I’ve designed 3 Career Quests for this week."
 ✅ “Polish your profile”
 ✅ “Post an Assignment Pulse”
 ✅ “Connect with 1 person in your domain”



Every Quest has:


A purpose


A progress tracker


A Musk tip


A clear action button (Go →)


After completion → user earns:


Points, badges


New visibility boosts


Sometimes, new unlocks



🧠 Why It’s Unique & Engaging
✅ AI-personalized: Quests change per user, per week, based on goals & profile progress
 ✅ Feels like a career RPG — users level up, grow, get feedback
 ✅ Social loop: Can showcase completion or badges on wall
 ✅ Musk gives feedback after each Quest: “Well done, your profile strength improved by 9%”
