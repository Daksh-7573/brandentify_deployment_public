Quantum Card – 3D Design (UI/UX in List View)
🔷 1. Layout & Card Structure
 3D layered card with depth and perspective tilt

 Card size: ~360x520 px

 Parallax tilt effect on cursor movement (for web) or phone tilt (for mobile)

 Multiple Z-indexed layers for floating elements

 Subtle shadow layers for visual depth

👤 2. Profile Picture
 Placed at the top center, popped out above the card surface

 Glowing circular frame with light reflection animation

 Slight hover bobbing effect to simulate floating

🧑‍💼 3. Name & Job Title
 Name

Large, semi-glowing font with light reflection overlay

Positioned centrally or upper-left for balance

 Job Title

Smaller text, floating below name

3D shadow or embossed effect

Optional label-style background with soft hover animation

🧩 4. Professional Info (Floating Panels)
 Industry / Domain

Displayed as floating glowing badges (e.g., #Marketing, #UIUX)

Soft floating animation with occasional pulse

 Company / Organization

🏢 icon + company name

Appears on a 3D card-in-card layer (like a business card inside the frame)

Subtle slide-in or lift animation

 Location

📍Icon + City, State

Positioned on a curved path or orbit-style ring (optional)

✉️ 5. Contact Information Layer
 Sits at the bottom layer of the card with a blurred glass effect

 Email Address

📧 icon + click-to-copy

Appears as an animated tag that rises into view on hover

 Phone Number

📞 icon + tap-to-call or copy

Displays like a holographic stamp at the corner of the card

🎨 6. Visual Theme & Styling
 3D Theme Base:

Soft glass with neon outlines OR

Matte clay texture with embossed fields

 Accent Colors:

Electric blue, silver gray, charcoal black, mint green

 Typography:

Futuristic (Orbitron, Sora, or Inter SemiBold)

Light spacing and subtle depth shadows

🧠 7. Interactions & Micro UX
 3D Tilt:

Reacts to mouse or device motion (rotateX, rotateY)

 Depth Glow:

Inner shadow/glow expands slightly on hover

 Animated Entrances:

Elements slide in or rise in layers when loaded

 Soft Sound FX (Optional):

Light “ping” when clicking copy buttons

🛠️ 8. Extras & Optional Features
 “View Full Profile” button rises from the base layer

 “Powered by Musk” hologram tag in bottom-right

 Optional animated background behind card (slow loop or particle float)
