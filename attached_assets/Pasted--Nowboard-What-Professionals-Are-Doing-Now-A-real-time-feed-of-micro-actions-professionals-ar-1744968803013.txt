“Nowboard” — What Professionals Are Doing Now
🧠 "A real-time feed of micro-actions professionals are taking — to inspire, connect, and act faster."

🎯 What is Nowboard?
It’s a live, minimalist, AI-filtered feed that shows what people across your industry are currently doing, sharing short snippets of:

🎓 “Completed a course on AI in Healthcare”

🚀 “Launched a case study on Web3 onboarding UX”

🧠 “Rewrote my resume to target growth PM roles”

📝 “Started drafting a post on burnout recovery”

📢 “Updated my portfolio with my new packaging design”

🤝 “Scheduled a chat with a hiring manager at X”

🛠️ How It Works
User gets a micro-input box:

“What did you do for your professional growth today?”
Musk even suggests based on activity:
“Want to share that project update you just uploaded?”

User selects a category (Growth, Learning, Launch, Planning, Collab, Visibility)

150-character max, no hashtags, just pure real-world moves

Appears on Nowboard Feed (public or connections-only)

🧠 What Makes It Useful
👀 Insight into what others are doing right now

🚀 Inspires users to take action (less thinking, more doing)

💡 Helps users document progress (build in public)

🧭 Makes professionals visible even without posting full Pulses

🧠 Musk can suggest what to do next:

“10 others in Product Design are creating new case studies. Want to try one?”

📊 Use Cases

Persona	How They Use Nowboard

Student	“Finished Week 3 of Data Science bootcamp. Feeling strong.”
