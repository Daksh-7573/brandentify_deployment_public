4️⃣ Improvement #1: Question Purpose Classification (Lightweight)

Right now you classify user intent.
Now also classify question purpose.

Add this enum (very small change):
type FollowUpPurpose =
  | 'clarify'
  | 'expand'
  | 'decide'
  | 'execute'
  | 'validate'
  | 'reflect';

Why this matters

Instead of:

“Which skills should I learn?”

You generate:

Clarify → “Which role are you targeting next?”

Decide → “Should you upskill or specialize deeper?”

Execute → “Want a 30-day skill plan?”

Validate → “Does this align with your long-term goal?”

👉 Same intent, better trajectory.
