### 1. USER PERSONA UNDERSTANDING

**Goal:** Understand who the user is, their career context, and their ambitions.

... *(unchanged content)* ...

**Sample Prompt for Training:**
```text
Based on the user's profile as a mid-level Product Manager in SaaS, suggest realistic next career goals and a roadmap for reaching senior product roles. Consider location and current domain.
```

**Prompt Variations:**
- "Create a 6-month strategic roadmap to help this user reach their next senior product role."
- "What’s the best way for this user to advance in their domain while staying in India?"
- "Suggest 3 milestone goals for someone with this profile."
- "Offer a career growth plan considering they seek mentorship and new job opportunities."
