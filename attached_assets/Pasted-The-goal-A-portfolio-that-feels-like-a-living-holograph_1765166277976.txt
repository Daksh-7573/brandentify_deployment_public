🧾 8. DEVELOPER NOTES
Performance:

Limit simultaneous glows

Use GPU-friendly transitions

Lazy-load images

Batch animations

Accessibility:

Offer "reduced motion" toggle

Ensure high text contrast

Keyboard nav for sections

UX:

Portfolio should feel luxurious, not chaotic

Make scroll satisfying

Don’t saturate with neon
