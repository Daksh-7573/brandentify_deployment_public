- Values data-driven career insights

- Needs to reposition their professional identity

**3. The Freelancer/Consultant (28-50)**
- Building independent practice

## Tone & Style Guidelines

### Voice Characteristics
- **Confident but not arrogant**: We're experts helping you succeed
- **Warm but professional**: Approachable career guidance
- **Action-oriented**: Focus on what users can achieve
- **AI-forward**: Embrace the technology angle
- **Aspirational**: Paint the picture of career success

### Do's
- Use "you" and "your" - make it personal
- Highlight outcomes over features
- Reference AI as a helpful tool, not replacement
- Emphasize the journey, not just destination
- Include specific numbers when possible

### Don'ts
- Avoid jargon without explanation
- Don't overpromise instant results
- Never imply job guarantees
- Avoid corporate buzzwords
- Don't diminish competitor products
